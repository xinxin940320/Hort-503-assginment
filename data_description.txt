!Series_summary	The wheat Nils expression profiles differ because of their response to aphid feeding (i.e., antibiosis - Dn1; tolerance - Dn2; antixenosis and antibiosis - Dn5)												
!Series_summary	We used GeneChip Wheat Genome Array (Affymetrix, USA) analysis to detail the global programme of gene expression underlying cellularisation and identified distinct classes of up-regulated genes during this process.												
!Series_overall_design	Plants of each cultivar were infested with 10 aphids and incubated for 48 h. All leaves except the first leaf were harvested into liquid N2 and stored at 280¬∞C prior to RNA isolation.
